Stock Prediction Application

To investigate the effectiveness of an investor using the knowledge, discovery, and data mining process to assist the creating and maintaining a Jamaica Stock Exchange stock portfolio. The project will seek to help answer the following questions an investor might have, in a systematic way void of emotion, that is, “What stocks to buy/sell”, “When to buy/sell” and “Why should I buy or sell?”.
